About Bohr Model

The Bohr model shows the arrangement of electrons in an atom, with electrons moving in specific orbits (shells) around the nucleus. Each shell can hold a maximum number of electrons (2, 8, 18, 32...). While simplified, this model helps visualize how electrons are distributed.
